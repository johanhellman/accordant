# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-10

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Accordant ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our internal LLM Council application, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services:

### A. Account Information
- **Username:** Used for identification and display purposes within your organization.
- **Password:** Stored as a cryptographic hash (we never see your actual password).
- **Organization ID:** Associates your account with your specific tenant.

### B. Usage Data
- **Conversations:** The messages you send and the AI responses generated.
- **Votes & Rankings:** Your feedback on AI personality performance, used to improve the system.
- **Interaction Logs:** Timestamps of when you create conversations or send messages.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We use your data for the following purposes:
- **Service Provision:** To enable chat functionality, manage your account, and maintain conversation history.
- **Authentication:** To verify your identity and secure your account.
- **System Improvement:** Aggregated voting data is used to rank and evolve AI personalities. This data is processed anonymously where possible.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing
We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:
- **Performance of Contract:** To provide the application services you have requested.
- **Legitimate Interests:** To improve our services, ensure security, and prevent fraud.

## 5. Data Storage and Retention
- **Storage:** Your data is stored on secure servers located within [Region, e.g., the EU].
- **Retention:** We retain your account information and conversation history for as long as your account is active.
- **Deletion:** You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see "Your Rights").

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)
Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- **Right to Access:** You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification:** You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** You can request the deletion of your personal data.
- **Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
- **Right to Object:** You can object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

**How to Exercise These Rights:**
You can exercise many of these rights directly within the application:
- **Access & Portability:** Navigate to **User Settings > Export Data** to download a copy of your personal information and conversation history.
- **Erasure:** Navigate to **User Settings > Delete Account** to permanently remove your account and data.
- **Rectification:** You can update your profile information in **User Settings**.

For any rights not exercisable directly through the settings, or for further assistance, please contact your Organization Administrator.

## 7. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. This includes password hashing and data segregation between organizations.

## 8. Cookies
We may use essential cookies or local storage tokens solely for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the application.
